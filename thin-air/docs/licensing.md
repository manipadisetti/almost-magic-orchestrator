# Licensing & Tiers

Thin Air offers flexible licensing options for individuals, teams, and enterprises.

## 1. Pro License (Individual)
Designed for solo developers and freelancers.

*   **Price**: $49/month
*   **Includes**:
    *   Unlimited Projects
    *   Access to all 5 Phases (Vapour to Manifest)
    *   Priority AI Processing
    *   Export to GitHub
    *   Standard Support
*   **Usage**: Single user only.

## 2. Team License
Perfect for small startups and agencies.

*   **Price**: $199/month (up to 5 users)
*   **Includes**:
    *   Everything in Pro
    *   Shared Workspaces
    *   Role-Based Access Control
    *   Team Asset Library
    *   Audit Logs
*   **Compliance**: SOC2, GDPR, HIPAA ready.

## 4. Educational / Non-Profit
We offer 50% off for students and registered non-profits. Contact support@almostmagic.com with proof of status.

---

## License Management
Admins can manage licenses via the **Admin Dashboard** (`/admin`).
*   **Assign Licenses**: Add users by email.
*   **Revoke Access**: Remove users instantly.
*   **Billing**: Centralized billing via Stripe.
